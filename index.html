A portfolio is a curated collection of work that showcases an individual's or organization's skills, accomplishments, and expertise in a particular field. It can take various forms, such as a digital website, a physical folder, or a multimedia presentation, depending on the industry and purpose. Portfolios are commonly used by professionals in creative fields like graphic design, photography, writing, and architecture, as well as by business professionals, investors, and job seekers to demonstrate their achievements and capabilities. A well-structured portfolio highlights the best work, provides context for each project, and helps build credibility and trust with potential clients, employers, or collaborators.
